Poverty has problems that arise from it such as unemployment but most notable is the rise in crimes that are done by people or groups of people.

But there are certain crimes that emerge from poverty, these crimes include, theft, murder, drug-use, and most common, the sale of drugs.

These crimes are committed by people in poverty so they can make money and make ends meet in what poor conditions they live in.
