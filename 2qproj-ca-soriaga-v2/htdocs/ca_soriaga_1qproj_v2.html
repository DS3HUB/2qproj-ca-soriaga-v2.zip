HOW CAN WE SOLVE POVERTY?
What is Poverty? What are the problems of Poverty? How do we deal with Poverty? How to not deal with Poverty Credits

Poverty is a state of which a person cannot afford basic needs or having a low income that cannot support his or her needs.

By basic needs this could mean being unable to obtain food or drinking water or being able to see a doctor or having no shelter or home.

But poverty can vary from country to country such as Russian Poverty to Philippine Poverty. Some countries have a worse form of poverty

such as being homeless with no access to food or water while other countries have a more mediocre sort of poverty in which the homeless

can access food or water but in limited quantities.
